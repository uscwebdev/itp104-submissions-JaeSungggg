So, who am I?

Greetings! I'm Jae Sung Park, a sophomore at the University of Southern California, passionately navigating the realms of Cognitive Science with a dash of Web Development and User Experience Design on the side. While my academic journey began with aspirations in architecture, my evolving ambitions have gracefully steered me towards the captivating world of UX Design. As a curious mind delving into the intricate landscape of Cognitive Science, I find joy in unraveling the mysteries of the mind and exploring the interconnectedness of human cognition and technology. This multidisciplinary approach enriches my perspective and informs my work in UX Design, providing a unique blend of analytical thinking and creative problem-solving.
